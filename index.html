A Day in the Life
of a Wedding Photographer



Start

  • 8:00am - Wake up.
  • 8:15am - Shower.
  • 8:30am - Coffee & Breakfast.
  • 9:00am - Check email.
  • 9:30am - Prepare equipment by cleaning and organizing camera bodies and lenses,
  • 10:00am - Double check the schedule and GPS.
  • 10:30am - Lay out gear, check cards, pack lunch/ water.

Middle

  • 11:00am - Pack gear up and load car.
  • 11:15am - Leave for Wedding.
  • 12:00pm - Arrive at church and meet with 2nd photographer.
  • 12:30pm - Begin photographing bride and bridesmaid preparation while second shooter will cover the groom and groomsmen.
  • 2:00pm - Ceremony begins.
  • 2:30pm - Ceremony ends.
  • 2:40pm - Family Photos.
  • 3:15pm - Leave for Location Photos.
  • 3:30pm - Arrive at forest park.
  • 5:30pm - Leave for Reception.

End

  • 6:00pm - Arrive at reception.
  • 6:15pm - Wedding party announced.
  • 6:25pm - Cake Cutting.
  • 6:45pm - Welcom/Blessing.
  • 7:00pm - Dinner.
  • 7:45pm - Speeches.
  • 8:00pm - First dance.
  • 8:05pm - Parent dance.
  • 8:15pm - Sunset Photos.
  • 8:30pm - Pack up and leave.
  • 9:00pm - Arrive home.
  • 9:00pm - Review and Backup photos.
  • 10:00pm - Sleep.
